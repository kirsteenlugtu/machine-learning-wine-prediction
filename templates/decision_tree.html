The Decision Tree Model

was used to determine the possible quality of the red and white wines by continously splitting their different properties' results into nodes until classified.

This model was intended to be easy to visualize but there were too many properties of the wines to have clear visualizations as seen below.

The decision trees did not generalize the data well & was not the most accurate in determining quality for red or white wines.
